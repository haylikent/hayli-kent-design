NE Writing Project

Branding and Campaign

Print | Custom Type | Environmental

Identity system and promotional campaign for the Nebraska Writing Project, which attracts high school students in search of safe space to share their thoughts, opinions, and creative questions. Emulating traditional media to feel explorative, individualistic, and rebellious, the Nebraska Writing Project works to break the standard classroom rules and keep students questioning the way things work.
