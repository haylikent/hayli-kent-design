Period Co.

Branding & Packaging

Adobe Dimension | Photoshop | Identity

Younger women are often still fighting with their insecurities while purchasing feminine hygiene products for themselves. Using all-natural, eco-friendly materials, and honest language that any woman can relate to, Period Co. works to break the stigma around periods. Using a more scientific and straightforward approach, they want to make the entire process as uncomplicated and painless for both the user and the planet.
