Wands for Wildlife

Website Design

UI | Illustrator

Fully responsive, promotional website design for the Appalachian Wildlife Refuge’s “Wands for Wildlife” campaign, which encourages the donation of old mascara wands to remove fly eggs and larva from the fur and feathers of wild animals. In order to bring this movement back into the social spotlight, and attract young down-to-earth women to donate to a good cause, the design explores organic shapes and clean typography to bring together aspects of nature, and cosmetics.
