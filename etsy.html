Etsy

Annual Report

20 Page annual report, combining imagery with information in a way that conveys Etsy’s individualism and their platform for people to share their unique creations and discoveries. The design explores clean studio photography that interacts with typography, emphasizing the craft and detail that goes into each product sold through Etsy, feeling purposeful, yet harmonious.
